# Privacy Policy

TurboDrop is designed for local file transfer and does not require a cloud account.

## What TurboDrop Stores

TurboDrop may create local files during normal operation:

- received files in `./received_files`
- temporary uploaded files in `./uploads`
- transfer resume state files with the `.turbo` suffix

These files stay on your machine unless you explicitly move or share them.

## What TurboDrop Does Not Collect

TurboDrop does not intentionally collect:

- account information
- analytics telemetry
- cloud backups
- external usage tracking

## Network Scope

TurboDrop is intended for local network use. Devices participating in a transfer exchange discovery and transfer metadata required for the session.

## Temporary Data

Browser-uploaded files are stored locally as temporary working files before transfer. Managed temporary uploads are cleaned up after the transfer attempt when possible.

## User Responsibility

You are responsible for:

- choosing trusted peers on your LAN
- protecting access to the host running TurboDrop
- deleting local temporary or received files when required by your environment
